Every shimmering phrase on a McCluster lyric page carries one of these markers — an original icon for a pattern the song is really about. Tap one mid-song and the record pauses to ask you a question. This page is the full legend.

The consent rules this system lives by

Every prompt is optional, anonymous, and shown in the open — nothing runs quietly in the background. Your answers are never stored or tied to you, no personal profile is ever built, and nothing here infers medical or clinical conditions or political beliefs. These markers exist for one reason: the songs are about the scroll, and the scroll is about you. For curiosity, not diagnosis.
