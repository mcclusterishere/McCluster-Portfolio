The Distribution
Grant.

One signature puts your catalogue on Our Street. Your songs stream on your page, every play writes your ticker's tape, and every sale lands on the record. You keep ownership of everything.

Plain words · 1 min
  • You keep your masters. This is a license, never a transfer — your music stays yours.
  • Non-exclusive. Keep Spotify, Apple, everything. This just adds Equity Uprise as a floor.
  • Walk-away clause. Revoke with 30 days written notice; your works come down.
The grant — full text

1 · The license. The artist named below ("Artist") grants McCluster Corp / Equity Uprise ("the House") a non-exclusive, revocable license to host, stream, display and sell the works listed below on the Equity Uprise platform and its embassy pages.

2 · Ownership. All masters, compositions and rights remain the Artist's. Nothing here transfers ownership, publishing, or any right beyond clause 1.

3 · The money. Revenue from the works moves per deal records both sides sign on the platform — one price to the buyer, the split disclosed inside, every payment on the record.

4 · The exit. Either side may end this grant with 30 days written notice (email suffices). The works come down; records of past deals stand, as ledgers do.

5 · The warranty. The Artist confirms they hold the rights to grant this license for every work listed.
