Matthew McCluster

Creative Director & Founder — McCluster Corp · Equity Uprise

Matthew McCluster is an American creative director, photographer, web designer, and songwriter working across Connecticut and Georgia. He is the founder of McCluster Corp and its civic fellowship Equity Uprise — work recognized in 2025 by the State of Georgia and the City of Bridgeport with official proclamations. He also owns and operates Whip Equipped LLC, a parts-licensed automotive venture he organized in Acworth, Georgia in 2024. His creative output spans digital marketing, media production, web and platform development, and the Here / PRIM3 music catalogue, and he is the architect of the Street Credit Bureau reputation platform.

Ventures & work

McCluster Corp is Matthew's creative and civic company — the home of the Equity Uprise fellowship, the Here / PRIM3 music catalogue, and the Street Credit Bureau. Equity Uprise is its civic-fellowship program, recognized by the State of Georgia and the City of Bridgeport. Whip Equipped LLC is his parts-licensed automotive venture in Acworth, Georgia, now the basis of a mini web series in production. As a songwriter and producer he records under the Here / PRIM3 catalogue (ISRC prefix QT6KV), and as a builder he designed and operates the Street Credit Bureau, a reputation platform published in the open.
