Why this matters

ASCAP OnStage and BMI Live pay writers for live performances of their own songs — bar sets, showcases, festivals, church programs. The claim windows are short and the forms want exactly what this packet holds. Log every show the night you play it, and the platform keeps your performance history on the record — the same history that feeds grant reports and booking credibility. This is part of the Equity Uprise workforce program: artists who know the business side keep the money they earn.
