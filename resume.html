Matthew McCluster

Creative direction, delivered end to end.

Matthew McCluster is a digital marketing and platform-operations specialist working across Connecticut and Georgia. Creative director, photographer, web designer, songwriter. He founded McCluster Corp and its Equity Uprise civic fellowship, recognized by the State of Georgia and the City of Bridgeport with official proclamations. He also owns and operates Whip Equipped LLC, the parts-licensed car dealership he organized in Acworth, Georgia in 2024, now the basis of a mini web series in production.
