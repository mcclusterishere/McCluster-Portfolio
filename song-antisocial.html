I'm antisocial

'cause I'm always on social

Antisocial is about being always on and never here. So we built the opposite into this page. It only moves when you slow down. Scroll fast and you miss it. Scroll slow, homie, and the details come alive, the same way life does when you finally look up from the feed.

That is the whole practice, and the whole point of this site. Not more noise. Presence, on purpose. The song names the loop out loud so you can feel it, then step out of it.

Every glowing dot is a real, documented pattern the feed pulls us into. Tap one. Notice it. Noticing is where wellness starts, and it is free.
