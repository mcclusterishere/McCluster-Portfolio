The Tithe

You collect your full rate your way — card, cash, invoice. When the job is done, you give a 10% tithe, on your honor, to the nonprofit that keeps this network free and verified. No commission wall, no payout delay, no platform tax.

The Equity

Or the network bets on you: your rate stays exactly yours, the network lists you at the network price, and the difference is your equity in the machine — it funds your page, your advertising, your development, and the artist behind you. You book through your portal, the platform does the collecting, you never chase a payment. Enrollment is a conversation — raise your hand.

Either door, the customer hears the same true sentence: "Part of every dollar you spend here builds the next artist." That's the equity part.
