Matthew McCluster is a creative director, photographer, web designer, and songwriter based in Connecticut and Georgia. He founded McCluster Corp and the Equity Uprise civic fellowship — recognized in 2025 by the State of Georgia and the City of Bridgeport — and is the architect of the Street Credit Bureau reputation platform.

Boilerplate — "About McCluster Corp"

McCluster Corp is a creative and civic company founded by Matthew McCluster, based in Connecticut and Georgia. A registered Connecticut public charity, it operates the Equity Uprise civic fellowship, the Here / PRIM3 music catalogue, and the Street Credit Bureau. More at streetcreditbureau.com.
