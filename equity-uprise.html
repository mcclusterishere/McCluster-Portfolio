Equity.
Then We Rise.

Equity Uprise is a movement, a fellowship, and a platform — policy, music, media, and community outreach fused into one civic operating system. It runs real campaigns on the public record, turns culture into organizing power, and puts a seat at the table in front of anyone with a talent to bring. Recognized by the State of Georgia and the City of Bridgeport, and registered as a Connecticut public charity.

What Equity Uprise is

A movement down the middle — and out the other side

The two-party fight keeps people divided and answering to the same central forces. Equity Uprise walks straight down the middle of the aisle and out the other side: a platform built to organize people around what they actually need, not which team they're told to pick.

Culture as organizing power

We organize with music and art as political activism — concerts, events, and content that educate and move people. Every anthem in the McCluster catalogue doubles as a tool: a song that carries a policy, a story, a call to act.

Your talent is the superpower

Whatever you're great at is what the movement needs. The Policy Fellowship is free — you tell the movement who you are, what you can do, and what you'd fix first. Your first act as a fellow is a vote.

The flagship case — Docket 516R

One power company. 7.3 miles of monopoles. Two cities that fought back.

Equity Uprise ran the fight against a major transmission build on the public record — from filing to a court that sent the case back for a rematch. The whole record is archived and explained in plain English, scored to the 516R Release.
