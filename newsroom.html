Newsroom

Recognition and milestones for Matthew McCluster, McCluster Corp, and the Equity Uprise fellowship — from the public record.

December 2025

State of Georgia recognizes Equity Uprise

Governor Brian P. Kemp issued a proclamation recognizing Georgia Youth Innovation & Civic Leadership Week, honoring the work of Matthew McCluster and the Equity Uprise civic fellowship.

December 10, 2025

City of Bridgeport names an Equity Uprise Human Rights Day

Mayor Joseph P. Ganim and the City of Bridgeport, Connecticut issued a proclamation naming an Equity Uprise Human Rights Day, recognizing the fellowship founded by Matthew McCluster.

October 28, 2025

McCluster Corp registers as a Connecticut public charity

McCluster Corp completed its Connecticut public charity registration (CHR.0069693) with the State of Connecticut.

July 2026

The Street Credit Bureau launches in the open

McCluster Corp published the Street Credit Bureau — a community reputation platform issuing a 0-to-1000 score from six pillars — along with its white paper, at streetcreditbureau.com.
