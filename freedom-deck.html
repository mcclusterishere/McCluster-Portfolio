Built for every pair of eyes

No fine print. Anywhere.

This deck — and the whole new site — is set in Atkinson Hyperlegible, the typeface created by the Braille Institute for readers with aging and low vision, with bold slab headlines and deep contrast.

Elders, deacons, and board members read every word without reaching for their glasses. For a congregation-rooted audience, that is strategy, not decoration.
