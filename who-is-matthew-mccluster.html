Who is Matthew McCluster?

The most-asked questions about Matthew McCluster — creative director, founder of McCluster Corp and Equity Uprise — answered plainly, from the public record.

Who is Matthew McCluster?

Matthew McCluster is an American creative director, photographer, web designer, and songwriter based in Connecticut and Georgia. He is the founder of McCluster Corp and its civic fellowship Equity Uprise, and the owner-operator of Whip Equipped LLC. His civic work was recognized in 2025 by the State of Georgia and the City of Bridgeport with official proclamations.

What is McCluster Corp?

McCluster Corp is the creative and civic company Matthew McCluster founded — home of the Equity Uprise fellowship, the Here / PRIM3 music catalogue, and the Street Credit Bureau. It is a registered Connecticut public charity (CHR.0069693).

What is Equity Uprise?

Equity Uprise is McCluster Corp's civic-fellowship program. It was recognized by the State of Georgia with a gubernatorial proclamation (Georgia Youth Innovation & Civic Leadership Week, December 2025) and by the City of Bridgeport with a proclamation naming an Equity Uprise Human Rights Day (December 10, 2025).

What is the Street Credit Bureau?

The Street Credit Bureau is a reputation platform Matthew McCluster designed and publishes in the open through McCluster Corp. It issues a 0-to-1000 street-credit score built from six pillars. It is a community reputation index — explicitly not a credit score in the FCRA sense and not a security.

What recognition has Matthew McCluster received?

In 2025, Matthew McCluster and Equity Uprise were recognized by the State of Georgia (a proclamation from Gov. Brian P. Kemp for Georgia Youth Innovation & Civic Leadership Week) and by the City of Bridgeport, Connecticut (a proclamation from Mayor Joseph P. Ganim naming Equity Uprise Human Rights Day). See the newsroom for the record.

Where is Matthew McCluster based?

Matthew McCluster works across Connecticut (including Bridgeport) and Georgia (including Acworth), in the United States.

Is Matthew McCluster a musician?

Yes. Alongside creative direction and platform work, he is a songwriter and producer recording under the Here / PRIM3 catalogue (ISRC prefix QT6KV).
