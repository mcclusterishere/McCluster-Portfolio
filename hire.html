Questions before you book

What exactly is the paid discovery call?

A focused working session where we scope your project — deliverables, timeline, and budget — so any quote I send is real, not a guess. It filters for serious clients on both sides, and the fee applies to your project if we move forward.

How fast can you deliver?

Depends on scope, but I keep a tight pipeline and offer same-day edit turnarounds on shoot work. We'll lock exact dates on the discovery call.

Do you travel, or work remote?

Both. I'm based between Connecticut and Georgia and shoot on location; web, strategy, and post are fully remote. Travel is quoted into the project.

Who owns the final work?

You do. Deliverables and usage rights are handed over on delivery — spelled out in writing before we start so there are no surprises.

What if we're not the right fit?

I'll tell you on the call and point you somewhere better. I'd rather pass than take a project I can't make great.
