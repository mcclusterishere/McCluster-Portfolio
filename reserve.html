The two colors, one law: credit earned by work — deals completed, bounties delivered, services rendered — can cash out to dollars; that is the platform paying for work. Credit purchased or gifted spends anywhere in the loop and stays in the loop. The ledger enforces the line; no button can cross it.

Reserve dollars are attested by the operator from the platform's bank and Stripe balances and updated with the books above. E-Up credit is closed-loop platform credit under the Equity Uprise nonprofit — not a deposit, not a security, not a floating asset.
